Tiger

The tigers unique striped pattern helps it camouflage in the wild, making it a stealthy predator.

Scientific Name: Panthera tigris

Average Length: 2.7 meters

Average Lifespan: 10-15 years

Habitat: Forests, grasslands, and wetlands

The tiger is known for its powerful build and beautiful orange coat with black stripes. It is one of the largest big cats and plays a vital role in maintaining the balance of its ecosystem.
